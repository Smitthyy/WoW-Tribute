World of Warcraft

The game that changed a million lives

Here's a brief timeline of the World of Warcraft universe

  • 2004 World of Warcraft (Vanilla) released. This was the original retail version of the game.
  • 2007 The Burning Crusade released. This was the first expansion to be released and introduced new races of Blood Elves, Draenei and the Outland area to the map.
  • 2008 Wrath of the Lich King released. The second expansion of the series introduces the Death Knight class and the Northrend quest area.
  • 2010 Cataclysm released. The third expansion introduces Goblins, Worgen classes and Kezan, The Maelstrom, Twilight Highlands & Gilneas areas to the map.
  • 2012 Mists of Pandaria released. World of Warcrafts fourth expansion introduces the Pandaren race and the questing island of Pandaria.
  • 2014 Warlords of Draenor released. No new races were released for this expansion but it bought with it the questing area Draenor.
  • 2016 Legion released. No new races were released however a new playable class Demon Hunter was made available. Argus & The Broken Isles were the new questing areas available to play
  • 2018 Battle for Azeroth released. For this expansion several new classes were introduced; Void Elves, Lightforged Draenei, Dark Iron Dwarves, Kul Tiran, Mechagnome, Nightborne, Highmountain Tauren, Mag'har Orc, Zandalari Troll & Vulpera. Also introduced were the areas of Zandalar, Kul Tiras & Nazjatar.
  • 2020 Shadowlands released. No new races released for players this time, The Shadowlands is the new playable questing area.
